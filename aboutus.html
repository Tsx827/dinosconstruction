About Us

Locally owned and operated, Dino's Construction is a small local business serving primarily the Merrimack Valley and Southern NH area.

A little about the owner, Dean McComish Jr.

Growing up in Methuen, MA Dean now resides in Derry, NH. He is a family man who enjoys his work and spending time with family. He attended Whittier Regional Vocational Technical High school, where he studied carpentry, and worked a co-op job provided by the school. Shortly after graduating Whittier, Dean began working full-time for Black Dog Inc. Meanwhile he attended Northern Essex Community College where he studied business management.
