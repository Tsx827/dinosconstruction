About Us

Dino's Construction was created in 1999, by Dean McComish Jr. Locally owned and operated, Dino's Construction is local business serving the Merrimack Valley and Southern NH area.

A little about the owner, Dean McComish Jr.

Dean grew up in Methuen, MA and now resides in Derry, NH. Dean is a family man who cares about the customer business relationship. Dean attended Whittier Regional Vocational Technical High school, where he graduated with a certificate in carpentry. At a young age he began hands-on work in the construction industry. Shortly after graduating Whittier, Dean began working full-time for Black Dog Inc. while he also pursuded an associates in business management at Northern Essex Community College.
